 WHY THE DELAY

WHY CREDITS ARE USED
• Apollo charges 1 credit per contact when we fetch their email. So the credits you see = number of contact emails we enriched (e.g. today was around 576 credits = ~576 contact emails fetched). We’ve now reduced waste by filtering by job title first and only enriching matching contacts.

WHAT WE FIXED
• 50 companies → 29/38 contacts: We improved search and added a fallback so you get more contacts per company when Apollo has them.
• Level 2 & Level 3 count mismatch: We now save and show only contacts that match your chosen titles (CEO, HR, Director, etc.), so the numbers match in both levels.
• Level 3 now auto-loads your batch when you open Send Campaign—no need to select from the dropdown.
• Removed the contact count from the batch dropdown to avoid confusion.

Please also update your Apollo billing details—the “Payment Failure” message is from Apollo and needs to be fixed in their billing settings to keep your account active.

HOW TO SEE YOUR CONTACTS IN APOLLO (e.g. ITMUMBAI)
We tag each contact with the industry you enter. That value is in a custom field. In Apollo People use "More Filters" → your custom field "Industry" → set to your tag (e.g. ITMUMBAI). Do NOT use "Industry & Keywords" or "Company Keywords" — those show 0 for our tags. If you have not created the Industry custom field yet, see APOLLO_INDUSTRY_TRIAL.md.
